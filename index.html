The Reason I Made This Learning Tool

Since my early teens I started to realize that nature was an amazing phenomenon to behold. From how the bees pollinate the trees, to how the trees take in carbon-dioxide and give out oxygen for living organisms to have life; it all seemed too intelligent

This made me curious and I started asking some questions like, "How did it all begin?", "What is the purpose?", "How can the tiniest cell be so complex?", just to share a few.

I started looking into science for answers and I was finally convinced about a couple of things. First, that I love science and am thankful for all we know and have achieved with it, and second, that science did not have an absolute answer to certain fundamental questions such as the ones mentioned above. Personally, it did not satisfy my logic. It seemed bias at times and strongly opinionated. I know this is not the case for some but it surely was the case for me.

To find logic I decided to start looking in places that many may call illogical. I started looking into the spiritual, the metaphysical. I looked into or joined different religions, schools of thought, philosophies, occult groups and disciplines.

Through personal experiences and study, I finally was able to find the logical satisfaction I was missing to answer many fundamental questions. There is intelligence in all things, both animate and inanimate. Energy itself is intelligent and is in everything visible and invisible. There are laws not visible or tangible to us acting upon what we call reality at every moment. It's more complex than a composer writing a symphony playing above another symphony which are playing different songs in different keys yet somehow all working in harmony and/or complimenting each other to create a magnum opus composition.

This does not just happen without deliberation but it was created with super intelligence and precision. This intelligence was, is, and always will be behind the math, laws of nature, physics, and metaphysics which is creating all things at every present moment.

This lead me to respect what no man can fully explain and in-turn if not fully explainable, to fear in a healthy way this unknown intelligence. The need to know more made me search deeper to find out that there is infinitely more to search. To be humbled by the magnitude of pure Love that manifests itself around us through pure perfection that for most if not all the time we are oblivious to.

All these paths lead me to not believe in a Creator, but to know there is a Creator.

I know the above is a bold statement to make. Rest assured that by saying the above does not mean I am enlightened in some way, or that I am better in any way than someone else, or that I do not stumble at times and fall. No!

What this means is that I am at peace internally and strive for Godly perfection because this perfection is the manifestation of pure Love itself. This is how the Creator's pure Love is made manifest to us, it's through Godly perfection shown through all that the Creator has created. In-turn it's through us striving for Godly perfection (keeping the Law, whether all 613 Laws if Jewish or the 7 Noahide Laws if not Jewish) that we show our pure Love to the Creator who gave us the ability through free will to Love Him* in the first place.

*Note: I used the word "Him" in the above paragraph for lack of a better word in English. Just know the Creator is beyond the sexes, as the Creator is infinitely undefinable. The Creator is beyond good and evil, beyond male or female, beyond duality completely, as the Creator created all of these things. Remember all that exists, visible and invisible stemmed from the Creator and not the other way around.

The major thing that struck me the most throughout my experience with many major religions and occult groups, is that at some point the Hebrew scriptures, or letters, or the Kabbalah, are quoted or studied and yet these organizations are not Jewish. It made me ask an important question, "Why are these organizations who are not Jewish, so invested in Hebrew teachings and traditions in one way or another and yet are indifferent to learning the Hebrew language and claim (of course not directly but indirectly in some cases) that they know more about the hidden meaning behind the Hebrew words or letters than the Jewish Rabbis?"

This was and is illogical to me. How could some of these organizations without knowing the Hebrew language themselves at all in most cases, could so easily dismiss the authority and teachings of those who are masters at it? How could any of these organizations claim to have some special secret knowledge above and beyond the Jewish Rabbis?

It's complete arrogance or blindness to believe to know more than the people that since birth are subjected to the Hebrew language and traditions. Even more, it is ignorance to claim to know more than those that in their adolescent years learn to read it in school and use it daily. But, it's complete foolishness to say at any point to know more than those who have chosen to commit their whole life to studying, teaching, and observing the Hebrew scriptures, letters, and traditions over thousands of years generation after generation.

It is by logical thinking that if anyone wants to began to acquire a deeper understanding of any Hebrew traditions or the Creator as know through Judaism, that one should take the first step by learning how to read the Hebrew language. Then after learning how to read it, start learning the meaning of the words later. If learning the meaning of the words for spiritual reasons, it is suggested to study the meaning of the words under a Rabbi that is teaching it already.

As I do not know how to read Hebrew. This is what I have set out to do for myself. This tool I have created and am sharing for free with everyone is a tool I created to help me in my pursuit of learning more about life, love, and the Creator.

Though I am more than blessed and humbled to be able to share this tool for free. I only ask if you have used this tool and it has helped you in anyway shape or form. Please share your experience with others and show them where to find it.

Please keep in mind that it did take my time, knowledge, and skills to create this helpful tool that I am sharing and though not obligatory, I would be grateful for any donations you send my way.

If you wish to donate you can click on the donate word link or the donate button down below.
Thank you!
